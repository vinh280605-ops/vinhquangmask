From Necessity to Comfort

At Vinh Quang Mask, we noticed a problem: itchy fabrics, painful ear loops, and breathless designs. In a world where masks have become an essential part of daily life, we asked ourselves: "Why does being safe have to feel so uncomfortable?"

We decided to change that. Our mission became simple: To create a premium mask that you can wear from morning to night without even noticing it's there.

"Combining advanced filtration technology with skin-friendly materials, VQ Mask is designed to protect you and your loved ones, comfortably."

Safety Meets Softness

We don't just make masks; we engineer comfort. Here is what makes VQ Mask different.

Skin-Friendly Fabric

Premium non-woven fabric that is hypoallergenic and incredibly soft. No more acne or skin irritation, even after 8 hours of use.

Meltblown Protection

The core of our mask is the high-grade Meltblown layer, effectively filtering out 99% of bacteria and fine dust (BFE > 99%).

Pain-Free Ear Loops

Our ergonomic 4D design fits your face perfectly, while the extra-elastic ear loops ensure zero ear pain all day long.

Certified Safety
